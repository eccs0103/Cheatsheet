Settings

Theme

External design of the page: color scheme, background and foreground, font.

Incorrect cases

Show or hide incorrect cases while searching.
Enabling this option increases the CPU load.

Ignore case

The search will ignore case differences. Lower case will be read the same as upper case.

Skip words

Gives the option to skip words in the search, but the order of the words must be preserved.
Example: A search for "human yourself" will find a result in the sentence "Preserve the human being within yourself."
Enabling this option increases the CPU load.

Experimental functions

Toggles experimental mode. Experimental features may be unstable or malfunction.
